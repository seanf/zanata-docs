Chapter 7. View translation project status in Zanata

Procedure 7.1. Viewing translation status
  1. Log in to the Zanata instance.
  2. Click Projects
  3. Locate your translation project, and click it
  4. On your project's page, click "View Status" next to the project version you want to check.
    Result:
    You are taking to the statistics page for that version of your project. Here you can see how many words have been uploaded, and view the percentage completion for each of the target languages.
